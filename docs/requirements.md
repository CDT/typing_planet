# 打字星球 (Typing Planet) Requirements

## Overview

Typing Planet is a pure frontend application for helping K-12 students improve their typing skills. The product should feel simple, polished, engaging, and rewarding, with an experience that is especially appealing to teenagers.

## Target Users

- Primary audience: K-12 students, especially younger learners and teenagers.
- Secondary audience: parents or teachers who may introduce the tool to students.

## Product Goals

- Make typing practice approachable for beginners.
- Keep practice sessions short, focused, and enjoyable.
- Encourage repeat use through clear progress, feedback, and lightweight game-like motivation.
- Maintain a clean, elegant interface that avoids unnecessary complexity.

## Experience Requirements

- The application must be fully frontend-based.
- The interface should be simple to understand without onboarding friction.
- Visual design should be attractive to students while remaining clear and usable.
- Interactions should feel responsive, smooth, and rewarding.
- The experience should support sustained practice without feeling repetitive or tedious.

## Technical Requirements

- Provide Progressive Web App (PWA) support.
- Support deployment to Tencent Cloud EdgeOne.
- Keep the architecture suitable for static frontend hosting.

## Non-Goals

- Backend services are not required for the initial scope.
- Native mobile applications are not required for the initial scope.
